Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kartigo is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order
  • Order history and transaction details
  • Device and browser information for improving our website

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To contact you regarding your order status
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send order confirmations and updates via WhatsApp or SMS

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to fulfill your order (e.g., delivery partners) or comply with legal obligations.

Payment Security

Online payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store your card details. All transactions are encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant.
