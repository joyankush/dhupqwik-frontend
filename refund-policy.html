Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We want you to be happy with your purchase. Please read our return policy carefully before ordering.

Clothes — 4 Day Return Window

  • Returns accepted within 4 days of delivery for clothing items
  • Item must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with tags intact
  • Contact us within 4 days of receiving the item to initiate a return
  • Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the item

Gifts — Same Day Return

  • Gift items must be returned on the same day of delivery
  • Item must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Contact us immediately upon delivery if there is any issue

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been used, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Items returned after the return window has passed

How to Request a Return

Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 82502 21552 or email kartigo@gmail.com with your order ID and reason for return.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a photo. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.
